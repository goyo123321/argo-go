About Our Society

Investigating the Unexplained Since 2012

The Campus Paranormal Society was founded in 2012 by a group of students fascinated by the many unexplained occurrences on our university grounds. Our mission is to scientifically investigate paranormal claims while maintaining respect for those who may be affected by these phenomena.

We use a combination of traditional investigation methods and cutting-edge technology to document and analyze paranormal activity. All our findings are meticulously documented and shared with the university community through this blog.

Scientific Approach

We use EMF detectors, thermal cameras, and audio analysis in all investigations.

Community Driven

We welcome reports from all students and staff about unusual experiences.

Documented History

We maintain an archive of all campus paranormal reports since 1950.
